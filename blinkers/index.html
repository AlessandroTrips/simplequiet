Why it's different

Built so it can't spy, not just promises it won't.

No history access

Blinkers requests no permission to read your browsing history or the pages you visit. It literally can't see them.

No network calls

The free version makes zero calls to any server. Your blocklist, timer, and stats stay on your machine.

Blocking done locally

Sites are blocked by Chrome's own rules engine on your device — no traffic is routed through us, ever.
